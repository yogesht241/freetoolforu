How to Use the 7th CPC Salary Calculator

Welcome to our stunning 7th CPC Salary Calculator! Follow these simple steps to get your salary breakdown in seconds:

  • Select Your Pay Level: Pick your pay level based on your role (e.g., Faculty with AGP or Other Employee with GP) to set the base for your calculation.
  • Enter Your Basic Pay: Type your current basic pay in the field provided—it’s the starting point for your salary.
  • Choose City Type for HRA: Select your city (Metro, Non-Metro, Rural, or No HRA) to calculate your House Rent Allowance based on location.
  • Choose City Type for TA: Pick your city type (Metro, Non-Metro, Rural, or No TA) to determine your Transport Allowance, a fixed amount by location.
  • Select DA Percentage: Choose the current Dearness Allowance (e.g., 53% as of July 2024) to adjust for inflation in your salary.
  • Click Calculate: Hit the “Calculate Salary” button to see a detailed, colorful breakdown of your salary components!

That’s all it takes! Your results will appear instantly, and you can revisit this guide anytime for help. Enjoy the brilliance of our calculator!
